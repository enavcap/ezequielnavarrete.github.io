About Me

As a composer and arranger, I blend classical training with modern sensibilities to create compelling musical experiences. My work spans across various media, including film, television, and concert music.

With a background in orchestral composition and a passion for innovative sound design, I strive to push the boundaries of what's possible in contemporary musical storytelling.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with talented musicians and creators, contributing to projects that challenge and inspire me to grow as an artist.
